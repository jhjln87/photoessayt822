Monotonous

A photo essay by John Wendt



Monotonous...
Life becomes that way. The mornings are the same. Once the chore begins, leaving is the only thing on mind. And once it is over, the sun is down. What little time left is wasted with mindless scrolling.
Monotonous.

Life has become monotony.

It doesn't seem to end.

Too much time has passed, without even seeing the sun.

Life is monotony.

Getting ready for work. Today entails two meetings, one project proposal, and hours of monotony.

Time goes on.

With the sun down, the only light comes from the night owls.
